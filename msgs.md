What's built into the system

The system incorporates five eexplained inmaterials/LEARNING_METHODS.md):

1. Karpathy Feedback Loop — eved systematically, not ignored
2. Spaced Repetition + Active Recall — you retrieve words, you don't re-read them
3. Swain's Output Hypothesis — 50%+ of every session is you producing German
4. Krashen's i+1 — material is calibrated just above your current level
5. Deliberate Practice — every session targets a specific weakness, not comfort zone
