## CONNECTORS — The B1 difference-maker

Using these correctly signals B1 level. Learn them all.

### Adding
- und — and
- auch — also
- außerdem — moreover, in addition
- dazu / zudem — in addition
- darüber hinaus — furthermore
- nicht nur…, sondern auch… — not only…, but also…

### Contrasting
- aber — but
- jedoch / allerdings — however
- trotzdem — nevertheless
- obwohl + Nebensatz — although
- obgleich — although (more formal)
- während — while / whereas
- zwar…, aber… — it is true that…, but…

### Cause and effect
- weil + Nebensatz — because
- da + Nebensatz — since/because (more formal)
- denn — because (main clause follows)
- deshalb / deswegen / daher — therefore, that's why
- nämlich — namely / you see (explains reason, sits in middle of sentence)

### Concession
- trotzdem — nevertheless (main clause)
- obwohl — although (subordinate clause)
- zwar X, aber Y — X is true, but Y

### Purpose
- damit + Nebensatz — so that
- um…zu + Infinitiv — in order to

### Time
- als — when (single past event)
- wenn — when/if (repeated past or present/future)
- bevor — before
- nachdem — after
- seitdem — since (time)
- bis — until
- sobald — as soon as
- während — while

### Example / specification
- zum Beispiel (z.B.) — for example
- nämlich — namely
- und zwar — specifically / that is
- wie zum Beispiel — such as

### Summary / conclusion
- insgesamt — overall
- zusammenfassend — in summary
- schließlich — finally / after all
- letztendlich — ultimately
